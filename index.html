Coop Co Help Menu

Coop Co's main currency is $$$ which is generated based off the egg's value, current chicken count and egg laying rate.

The main upgrade system of Coop Co is the standard Common Research which is purchased with $$$, and costs increase quite rapidly at some point you may be able to automate its purchasing.

Prestige is the first main reset of the game that unlocks at Rocket Fuel Eggs, Prestige rewards Soul Eggs on reset which can be used in Epic Researches and they provide a special earnings boost based off of best Prestige rewards Soul Eggs and current Prestige rewards Soul Eggs.

Epic Research is the second set of researches in game, they provide special permanent bonuses, automation for boring tasks and QoL things to keep you sane at the cost of Soul Eggs

Eggspeditions add 6 discoverable planets each with their own egg, special challenge and boost reward, it is recommended to start with the Chicken Gain Boost planet first as the price to discover planets increases rapidly.

Contracts are a simple system they force a reset on your farm and you're stuck in the contract's assigned egg until you reach the $$$ goal, upon successfully completing a contract you will recieve Prophecy Eggs which boost contract rewards, difficulty and provide a massive multiplicative boost to Soul Eggs.

Coop Co achievements are the best way to keep track of your progress through the game, they don't provide any boosts or special unlocks (for now) but they give a "sense" of accomplishment.

Ascension is the 2nd major reset of Coop Co and it resets all previous progress in the game in exchange for Knowleggs (funny egg pun), which can be used for the next topic Legendary Research, as well this reset unlocks ascension tier contracts.

Legendary Research much like Epic Research, gives special permanent bonuses, Automators and QoL Upgrades in exchange for your precious Knowleggs.

Gemology is unlocked quite a bit later in the ascension reset but provides an interesting mechanic
